About Me

I was born in a small city in the Northern Philippines and moved to New York, USA at a young age. As a self-taught baker and artist, my journey started at a young age when I didn't have much creativity or money to spend on expensive cakes and treats.

In 2002, I started learning how to work with edible mediums and techniques. I run a successful online marketing agency where I learned how to have a profitable business in the US and have been in the cake space.

Every cake tells a story, and I'm here to help you tell yours through delicious, beautiful creations that bring joy to every celebration.
